Help & Returns.

Common questions about orders, shipping, quality, and returns. Can't find what you need? Write to us.

Orders & Payments
We begin processing orders within a few hours of confirmation. If you need to make a change, write to care@nazrani.com or call 98840 90151 as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate the request, but cannot guarantee changes once the order has been picked and packed.
We accept UPI, all major debit and credit cards, and net banking via our secure payment gateway. Cash on delivery is not available at this time.
Check your spam or promotions folder first. If it's not there, log in to your account and visit My Orders — your order will be listed there with all the details. You can also reach us at care@nazrani.com with your registered email address and we'll resend the confirmation.
Shipping & Delivery
Orders are dispatched within 2 business days. Delivery typically takes 4–7 business days depending on your location. Remote pincodes may take an additional 2–3 days. You will receive an airway bill number on your confirmation page to track the shipment in real time.
Yes — orders above ₹999 ship free anywhere in India. For orders below this threshold, shipping is calculated at checkout based on the total weight and your delivery pincode.
Your airway bill number (AWB) is shown on your order confirmation page. Use the "Track shipment" button there, or enter your AWB directly on the India Post or IndiaShipments tracking portal. If you've lost your confirmation link, find the AWB under My Orders.

Returns & Refunds
We stand behind every lot we ship. If you are not satisfied with your order for any reason — quality, freshness, or otherwise — write to us within 7 days of delivery at care@nazrani.com with your order number and a brief description of the issue. We will arrange a replacement or a full refund, whichever you prefer, with no questions asked.
We're sorry to hear that. Please take a photo of the damaged packaging or product and email it to care@nazrani.com along with your order number. We will dispatch a replacement within 2 business days or issue a full refund — your choice. You do not need to return the damaged item.
Once we confirm the refund, it is processed within 1–2 business days. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3–5 working days to reflect in your account.
Products & Quality
Every product in our catalogue is sourced from a single named location — a cooperative, estate, or village farm — not blended from multiple regions. The origin is stated on each product page so you know exactly where your food comes from. Read more on our Origins page.
Yes. We do not add synthetic colours, flavours, or preservatives to any product. Spices are cleaned, dried, and packed as-is. Honey is raw and unfiltered. Oils are cold-pressed without chemical solvents. Our Sourcing Standard details the full five-check process every incoming lot goes through before it's accepted.
Store spices and powders in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight — an airtight container is ideal once opened. Honey can be stored at room temperature; do not refrigerate. Cold-pressed oils should be kept away from heat and used within the shelf life printed on the pack.
